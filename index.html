Featured Players

Rohit Sharma

Indian Cricketer, Captain of the Indian Cricket Team in ODIs and T20Is, known for his explosive batting and world record of three double centuries in ODIs.

Virat Kohli

Indian Cricketer, Former Captain, widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world, known for his consistency and aggressive play style.

MS Dhoni

Former Indian Cricketer and Captain, known for his calm demeanor and incredible leadership, leading India to multiple ICC trophies.

Player Profiles

Virat Kohli

Full Name: Virat Kohli

Date of Birth: November 5, 1988

Batting Style: Right-hand bat

Bowling Style: Right-arm medium

Teams: India, Royal Challengers Bangalore (IPL)

Career Stats:

  • ODI Matches: 275
  • ODI Runs: 12,169
  • ODI Centuries: 71
  • T20I Matches: 99
  • T20I Runs: 3,227
  • Test Matches: 111
  • Test Runs: 8,676
  • Test Centuries: 29

Notable Achievements:

  • Captain of the Indian cricket team across formats.
  • Led India to the 2018 Test series victory in Australia.
  • Multiple ICC awards including ICC ODI Player of the Year.

Rohit Sharma

Full Name: Rohit Gurunath Sharma

Date of Birth: April 30, 1987

Batting Style: Right-hand bat

Bowling Style: Right-arm off-break

Teams: India, Mumbai Indians (IPL)

Career Stats:

  • ODI Matches: 258
  • ODI Runs: 10,948
  • ODI Centuries: 30
  • T20I Matches: 143
  • T20I Runs: 4,439
  • Test Matches: 103
  • Test Runs: 8,525
  • Test Centuries: 29

Notable Achievements:

  • Holds the record for the highest individual score in an ODI (264).
  • Successful captain of Mumbai Indians, winning multiple IPL titles.
  • Led India to a historic series victory in Australia in 2020.

MS Dhoni

Full Name: Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Date of Birth: July 7, 1981

Batting Style: Right-hand bat

Bowling Style: Right-arm medium

Teams: India, Chennai Super Kings (IPL)

Career Stats:

  • ODI Matches: 350
  • ODI Runs: 10,773
  • ODI Centuries: 10
  • T20I Matches: 98
  • T20I Runs: 1,617
  • Test Matches: 90
  • Test Runs: 4,876
  • Test Centuries: 6

Notable Achievements:

  • Captain of India’s 2007 T20 World Cup-winning team.
  • Led India to the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup and 2013 ICC Champions Trophy victories.
  • Renowned for his finishing abilities and wicketkeeping skills.
  • One of the most successful captains in the history of cricket.
